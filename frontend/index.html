What is BabyLLM?

BabyLLM is a minimal, self-learning NLP chatbot that starts with zero knowledge and evolves purely through interaction. Every correction you give becomes a new memory, and once enough information is gathered, it automatically retrains itself.

Think of it as an intelligent blank slate — perfect for experimenting with real-time learning, language understanding, and custom training. It's more than a chatbot — it's your personal AI experiment.
